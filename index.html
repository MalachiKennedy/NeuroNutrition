Understanding Neurodivergence

Neurodivergent individuals often face unique challenges when it comes to nutrition. Sensory sensitivities, executive function difficulties, and altered hunger cues can make meal planning and eating consistently challenging.

NeuroNutrition bridges the gap between neuroscience and nutrition, providing tools that accommodate these differences rather than fighting against them.

Our approach is built on understanding that there's no one-size-fits-all solution. We empower you to discover what works for your unique brain.

  • Texture-based meal filtering for sensory needs
  • Executive function support with prep time estimates
  • Energy level matching for realistic meal planning
  • Safe foods database for comfort and reliability
  • Nutrition education tailored to neurodivergent learning
  • Privacy-focused with no judgment or tracking shame
